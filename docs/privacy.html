Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Crypto Mining Calculator, a simple, fully client-side web utility designed to help users estimate cryptocurrency mining profitability. This tool runs entirely in your browser with no server interaction. We prioritize your privacy by ensuring that no personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted at any time. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to data protection and explains our practices in detail.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

As a static application, the Crypto Mining Calculator does not gather any information from users. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): No names, email addresses, or contact details are requested or stored.
  • Usage or Analytics Data: No IP addresses, browser types, device information, or location data is logged.
  • Input Data: Values entered for hash rate, difficulty, block reward, coin price, power consumption, or electricity cost remain strictly on your device.
  • Tracking Data: No cookies, pixels, or similar technologies are used to monitor activity.

All calculations occur locally using JavaScript, ensuring your inputs never leave your browser.

3. How the Tool Operates

The calculator processes inputs client-side:

  • Local Computation: Profit estimates are generated instantly in the browser without external requests.
  • No Persistence: Data is not saved across sessions unless you manually use browser features.
  • Static Hosting: Deployed on platforms like GitHub Pages, there is no backend to receive or store information.

This design provides complete privacy and offline capability after initial load.

4. Third-Party Services

We include Bootstrap via CDN for styling and layout. These resources are widely used and do not enable user tracking in this context. No analytics providers, advertising networks, or data-sharing services are integrated.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The tool is completely stateless and does not use cookies, localStorage for persistence, or any tracking mechanisms.

6. Data Security

Since no data is collected, there are no servers to breach. Users should maintain their own device security and use trusted networks.

7. Children's Privacy

The tool is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. International Compliance

Our no-collection approach complies with privacy regulations worldwide, including GDPR and CCPA.

9. Policy Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted here with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

For questions, refer to the project's repository or associated channels.

Your privacy is fundamental—no data means no risks. Thank you for using our tool responsibly.
